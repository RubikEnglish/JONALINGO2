24. And there were evil men plotting against the emperor.



25. They crept up on him one day when he was alone, when his four sons were away ruling in the furthermost parts of the kingdom and his three daughters were down in the garden.

27. The evil men took the emperor to a tower in the middle of a wide, treeless plain.



28. The tower had only a single window, with an iron bar across the center.



29. The plotters sealed the door with bricks and mortar once the emperor was inside.
